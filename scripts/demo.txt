OpenMemoryProtocol — sample upload
==================================

This is a small text file you can drop into the demo gateway's web UI to
exercise the `text` schema and the universal `file.*` probes.

After upload you should see:
  - byte_size  — populated by file.size
  - sha256     — populated by file.sha256
  - mime       — populated by file.mime (likely "text/plain")
  - line_count — populated by text.line_count

Multi-tenant routing: dev-alice lands on shard A, dev-bob on shard B.
